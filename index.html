Biography

Rithik Sachdev is an undergraduate engineering student in the Computer Science department at Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science, Indore, currently completing his final year of studies. Previously, he was a software engineering intern at Aaiway, India, where he worked on the backend modules. He is the Overall Coordinator of Entrepreneurship-Cell, SGSITS, where he has been promoting the start-up culture among young enthusiasts. Recently, he led the team and conducted the largest flagship event, E-Summit'21, where several start-ups with their novel ideas participated and got funding and incubation from some of the esteemed investors and organizations. He has all brought the Annual Research Initiative, through E-Cell, to SGSITS. He has also secured the second runners up position in State and National level hackathons. He also has a keen interest in open source development and has made some significant contributions and progress.
