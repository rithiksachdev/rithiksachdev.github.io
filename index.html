About me

I am a highly motivated and skilled computer science engineer with a passion for machine learning, object-oriented programming and distributed systems. My interest in these fields was sparked during my time working at Nextuple Inc, where I gained valuable experience in developing cutting-edge solutions. As an associate software engineer at Nextuple, I worked on market-driven fulfillment solution, gaining experience in parallel processing, distributed systems, and cloud computing. I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead in this dynamic field and am committed to making a positive impact through my work.
